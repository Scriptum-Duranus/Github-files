And so you follow her through the twists and turns of the alleys.

She was sprightly despite her aged appearance. You commanded her to stop, but she would ignore you as if you had said nothing. Sometimes you'd be so close you could almost reach out and grab her, but every time she was just out of reach. Other times she was so far ahead you could only see her round the next corner. This chase continued for some time, until at last you see her enter some manner of store. Your deep breaths and the furstration of your pursuit are finally getting to you, and you grip the handle to your sword as you dash into the store.



You brace yourself for what's inside.
